Biography

I am a Senior Research Engineer at Seagate Technology. Before joining Seagate, I was an ORAU Postdoctroral Research Fellow. I received my Ph.D., M.Sc. and B.Sc. degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of Illinois at Chicago, University of Tehran, Iran and Shiraz University, Iran, respectively. I worked as an intern at Bell Labs (Mathematics of Networks and Systems Research Department) and Huawei R&D (Wireless Labs), in summer 2015 and 2016, respectively.
